About the App

YatriRakshak is a comprehensive travel safety platform that combines cutting-edge blockchain technology with AI-powered security features. Our system provides tourists with secure digital identity verification, instant SOS capabilities, intelligent geo-fencing, real-time anomaly detection, and streamlined E-FIR filing processes.

Blockchain Identity

Secure, tamper-proof digital identity verification using distributed ledger technology for enhanced tourist safety.

SOS & Geo-fencing

Instant emergency alerts with precise location tracking and intelligent boundary monitoring for tourist zones.

AI Anomaly Detection

Advanced machine learning algorithms that detect unusual patterns and potential threats in real-time.
